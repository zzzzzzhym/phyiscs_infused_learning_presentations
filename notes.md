## How-tos and lessons learned

This is the presentation slides and its material of Zhang, Yiming, and Junyi Geng. "Physics-infused Learning for Aerial Manipulator in Winds and Near-Wall Environments." AIAA SCITECH 2026 Forum. 2026.

For table with figures, this is a joint The materials are generated as below: 
- Equations: 
    - When working on google slides, making equations are difficult. One workaround is to use python to generate equations as figures and insert to the slides.
- Table of figures:
    - Table of figures are available in latex form. Use overleaf to generate a table of figures only pdf, then use an online pdf to png converter. 
- Diagrams:
    - Diagrams are made in draw io then convert to png. In draw io -> export as -> advance -> Use 1000dpi to ensure the max resolution.
